---
title: Organize Your Documentation
description: A short guide to structuring your Cassandra Crossno docs.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Sections">
    ````
    Use the dashboard to add sections like Overview, Guides, and API.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages to Sections">
    ````
    Click "Add Page" inside any section for easy navigation.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder Pages">
    ````
    Drag and drop pages to arrange your documentation order.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    ````
    Hit "Publish" when you're ready for others to view your docs.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
